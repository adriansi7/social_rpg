Social RPG

About

Social RPG is a role playing game which you can play if you login with a Facebook account.
The player can explore the game map, fight monsters and gain experience.

On the home page the user will be presented with our game's logo and a simple menu. More details below :



Menu

Login With Facebook - To start a new game you need to login with your Facebook accounnt.

Tell A Friend - Share our game to your friends

Exit - Close the browser webpage
